Our Story

Founded in 2024 by Zinhle Nhlapo & Shandukani Mudzwiri, RadiantRover was born out of a passion for beauty and a desire to make top-notch salon services accessible to everyone. With years of experience in the beauty industry, Zinhle & Shandu recognized a need for a more flexible, customer-centric approach to beauty treatments. Today, RadiantRover is a trusted name, known for our professionalism, attention to detail, and exceptional customer service.
